# Privacy Policy
Last Updated: March 2026

## Overview
The **Bypass Pro** browser extension is designed with a strict privacy-first approach. We believe that your browsing experience should be uninterrupted and your data should remain yours.

## Data Collection
**We do not collect any user data.** 
The Bypass Pro extension operates entirely on your local machine. It does not track the websites you visit, it does not record the content you read, and it does not transmit any information to external servers.

## Permissions Usage
The extension requests broad host permissions (`<all_urls>`) solely for the purpose of executing its core function: removing overlays on the active webpage you are visiting. The extension only injects its script locally within your browser and does not send any page data over the network.

- **Storage**: Used only to save your local extension preferences (e.g., whether the extension is toggled on or off).
- **ActiveTab & Scripting**: Used locally to modify the Document Object Model (DOM) of the page you are viewing to hide blocking elements.

## Third-Party Access
Since we do not collect any personal data, we do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer any information to outside parties.

## Changes to this Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
